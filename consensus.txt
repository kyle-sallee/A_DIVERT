By    minds           when bigoted among persons
equal regard
equal importance      is   abstained.

The   assistance      to   attain
to    consensus
some  importance must be   accorded or feigned.

Consensus  if denounced
assistance as declined         is tacitly threatened.
Comply  or be deprived  or die is tacitly threatened.

Liberty to    deprive   consensus when    wielded
as      peer  pressure            is      called.

Among free   minds consensus while not  empowered while not important
assistance   is    important and   sans demand    is        given.
